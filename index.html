About Me

Hey there! You can call me Blue. I am a young artist who enjoys digital art, collaging, watercolor painting, sculpting, and sewing. Art has always been my hobby, but one day, I'd like it to be my career too!

On top of art, I am also an avid collector and reader. I enjoy collecting PVC and vinyl figures, vinyl records, enamel pins, tin buttons, acrylic keychains, and plush keychains. I love reading horror and occult novels. I also enjoy reading fantasy, occult, and horror comic books. I prefer reading physical books over digital books or eBooks.

As for my friends, I have a black labrador-collie mix named Bear. I also have a partial albino corn snake (known as a Snowy Corn Snake) named Xerxes (ZERK-sees). Her partial albinism makes her look pink. On top of my animal friends, I have a small garden where I grow basil, eggplants, Japanese eggplants, Asian pears, oranges, lemons, and cherry tomatoes. I expect to have my first Asian pear crop this spring!
